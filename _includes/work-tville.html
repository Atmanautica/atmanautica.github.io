    In 2010, Facebook was just beginning its pandemic spread. Kids, restricted from joining by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, could play with their friends online illegally, or with strangers anonymously on platforms like Club Penguin. We offered them a safe place to play with their friends and families: games with leaderboards and dares, curated videos to like and share, quips (think: tweets), and art creation apps. Stretching to fit our needs, I developed our content strategy, designed flash games and art apps, made promo videos, conduct user research, prototyped new features and interaces, and started learning to code.
