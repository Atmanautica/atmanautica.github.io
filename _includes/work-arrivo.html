    Envisioning the end of traffic

    "Don't let Earth suck" sang the gargantuan neon sign at HQ. We assembled all-stars and avengers to put an end to traffic. The world might not be ready for a hyperloop, but aren't we ready to arrive?

    {%- include closer.html content="arrivo-thumb-closer" -%}

    It was a blessing and a joy to think so big. In an attempt to inform our ambitions, I created a comprehensive timeline of transportation technology, presented on an 80" touchscreen. Stretching to fit our needs, I architected the IT infrastructure, refreshed our website, coordinated our move into HQ, and juggled any number of odd operations duties. And that golf machine!
