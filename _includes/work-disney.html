    Blazing the trail to Disney+, Magic Bands, and Pokemon Go

    {%- include closer.html content="disney-thumb-closer" -%}

    We got off to a rocky start when Disney Interactive chose to sunset our Togetherville platform and all of our products and services within six months of our acquisition. But the silver lining appeared as we began to apply our technological acumen to dream up the magical (and plausible) wonders we could offer to the world via The Mouse.

    We soon secured a patent for the Dynamic Trust Score, then proceeded on to architect the integration of the company's identity platforms into what was termed Disney ID.

    Then the real fun began. In a few short months we spun up and pitched a squall line of concepts that have taken nearly a decade to come to fruition. Some highlights:

    • a precursor to Pokemon Go featuring collectible Disney characters living in AR,
    • a celestial Disney jukebox that became the Disney+ streaming service, and
    • an evolution of Disney Pins involving smart, wearable collectibles that are now called Magic Bands.
