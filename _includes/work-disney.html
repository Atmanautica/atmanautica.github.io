    Blazing the trail to Disney+, Magic Bands, and Pokemon Go

    We got off to a rocky start when they sunset our Togetherville platform and all of our products and services within six months after our acquisition. But the silver lining appeared as we began to apply our technological acumen to dream up the magical (and plausible) wonders we could offer to the world via The Mouse.

    While we were initially tasked with integrating the company's identity platforms into what was termed Disney ID, we spun up and pitched a rich storm of concepts: a precursor to Pokemon Go featuring collectible Disney characters living in AR, a celestial Disney jukebox that became the Disney+ streaming service, and an evolution of Disney Pins involving wearable smart collectibles that are now called Magic Bands.
