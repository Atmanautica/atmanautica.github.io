Show Me the Money

The more beautiful world our hearts know is possible operates without generalizing, commodifying, and monetizing what matters to us. That world sits just over the horizon, and in these times of transition and transformation, I have occasional and lessening needs that can be satisfied with money. If you find yourself with a surplus of money and wish to offer me a gift, I will gladly accept. You have my gratitude either way, for being, for being you, for being present now•here.
